Jonathan White, EA, MBA

Jonathan achieved his BS in psychology from the University of Georgia, and an MBA in accounting from Georgia State University. He worked as the principal accountant for a wholesale food manufacturer for four years. It was there that he realized he wanted to help other small businesses achieve their financial goals. As an enrolled agent, he can represent you for the IRS in both personal and business returns. He represents clients in many industries, but specializes in self- employed retail, manufacturing, food service and IT industries.
